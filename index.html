Have you been to the restaurant on The Mun? Good food, no atmosphere.

Founded January 2020 by entrepreneur, kerbonaut, and junkyard owner Jebediah Kerman, Jeb's Place is a classic American Burger Joint with Food That's Out Of This Galaxy!

Jebediah has temporarily put his desire for space exploration and junkyard salvage on hold to fix food. Countless hours orbitting Kerbin and the Galaxy's various celestial bodies has forced Jeb to live on KSP approved Space-Safe meals of questionable taste and safety for years. This ignited a passion for quality cooking, ingredients you can pronounce, and flavor that'll blow you away like a first-timer's out of control rocket crashing into the launch pad.

Come on down to Jeb's Place and hit the "Lunch Pad" where you can explore our Rocket Fuel Soda Fountain (non-combustible), order a Fly-By (take-out), grab a Moonshake, or taste-test Jeb's favorite Mystery Goo (Warning: not for human consumption). We even have a Meteorite Menu for the kids!

Jeb's Place is pleased to announce new Delivery options starting June 2020. Great food delivered fast by ICBM; so fast, you won't know what hit you!

Coming Soonish: An all new Vegetable Assembly Station (VAS) for Salad Bar lovers.
